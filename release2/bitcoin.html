Bitcoin - The Digital Gold

Bitcoin has doubled more than 5 times since it's release! It once sold for under $150 per coin, as of March 1, 2021, one Bitcoin now sells for around $50,000. It has a limited supply of 21 million coins, many expect its price to only keep rising as time goes on. Especially as more large, institutional investors begin treating it as a sort of digital gold to hedge against market volatility and inflation.
