Digital Currency? What Is That About?

Digital currencies dare not physical attributes and are only in digital form. Transactions involving digital currencies are made via the internet or elctronic trasnfers. In contrast, physical currencies, such as banknotes and minted coins, are tangible, meaning they have definite physical attributes and characteristics. For these physical currencies the uses are only made possible when their holders have physical possession of these currencies.

Digital currencies have use case similar to that of physical currencies. They can be used to purchase goods and pay for services. They can also find restricted use among certain online communities, such as gaming sites, gambling portals, or social networks.
