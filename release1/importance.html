Why Is Blockchain Important to the World?



All of the cryptocurrencies out in the world today are stored and trasnfered via the blockchain. The blockchain can be viewd as the "guide or goverening structure" of transactions. By using blockhain technology the transactions made by users can be easily tracked.



Blockchain helps in the verification and traceability of multistep transactions needing verification and traceability. It can provide secure transactions, reduce compliance costs, and speed up data transfer processing. Blockchain technology can help contract management (smart contracts) and audit the origin of a product (NFTs).
